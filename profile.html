About Me

Hello! My name is Siti Aisha binti Mohd Faisal, and I am 20 years old. I am currently pursuing my studies in the Faculty of Information Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM). As the eldest of five siblings, I have learned important lessons in leadership, responsibility, and time management skills that I apply in both my academic and personal life.

In addition to my studies, I had the opportunity to complete an internship at Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka (UTeM). During this work experience , I was able to apply the knowledge I gained in the classroom to real-world situations. I worked closely with a team to assist in various information management tasks. This hands-on experience not only helped me strengthen my skills in communication, problem-solving, and teamwork, but also gave me practical insight into how information is managed in a professional environment. It was a valuable experience that has further motivated me to pursue a career in information management.
